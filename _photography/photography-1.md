Location: China University of Mining and Technology, Beijing  
Date: 2018-2021 

A long time has passed, and now I can only find these precious photos in my history records. All the photos posted here were taken during competitions I participated in on behalf of the School of Earth Sciences and Surveying Engineering. In fact, most of my football experience came from casual pick-up games after class every day. The one with the red jersey marks the last match of my undergraduate studies back in 2021.
